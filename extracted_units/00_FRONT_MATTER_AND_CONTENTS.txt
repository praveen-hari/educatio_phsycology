PREFACE

This text book titled “EDUCATIONAL PSYCHOLOGY” . has
been written, based on the new syllabus for the I Semester
paper of the B.Ed. Degree Course of the Tamil Nadu Teacher
Education University. The five units of this subject has been
discussed under the following headings.

1, Educational Psychology and Human Growth Development,

2. Attention, Perception and Memory, 3. Motivation and
Learning, 4. Intelligence and Creativity, 5. Personality.

To facilitate the easy understanding of the subject by
the teacher-trainees, the contents have been presented under
different headings and subheadings. As of our previous works,
this book is also written in a lucid style with vivid explanations
and apt examples. To help students prepare for their
examinations, review questions and the expected answers for
the same have been provided at the end of each lesson. A set
of Model Question Papers based on the University examination
pattern with the expected answers for the same cited in the
book, have been provided at the end of this book to help
students who are to take their B.Ed. Degree Examination under
the new syllabus for the first time.

former Office Superintendent of Meston College of Education,
Chennai — 14, for his pains-taking efforts in proof reading this
book and Mr. C. Boopalan of Printech India for neatly printing
this book.
